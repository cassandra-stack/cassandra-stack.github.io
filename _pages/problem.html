The Challenge in Modern Oncology

Every year, over 300,000 people worldwide are diagnosed with brain tumors.

THE CURRENT REALITY:

4-6 hours of manual analysis per patient

Measurements vary between doctors by up to 30%

Limited capacity means patients wait weeks for results

Behind every scan is a patient waiting for answers. Every hour of delay is an hour of uncertainty.
